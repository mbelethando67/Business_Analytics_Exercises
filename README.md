# 📊 Business Analytics Terminology Assignment

## 📘 Overview

This project is a research-based glossary completed as part of the **Business Analytics — Foundations** course. The purpose of the assignment is to build a strong understanding of key terms, concepts, and performance metrics used in business analytics.

The assignment includes definitions of **38 essential terms** that explain how businesses use data to analyze performance and make informed decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand and correctly use core business analytics terminology
* Distinguish between commonly confused concepts (e.g., KPI vs Metric, BI vs Analytics)
* Apply each concept to real-world business situations
* Develop foundational knowledge for data-driven decision-making

---

## 🧠 Structure of the Assignment

The glossary is divided into four sections:

### 1. Foundational Concepts of Analytics

Covers core ideas such as:

* Business Analytics and Data Analytics
* Business Intelligence (BI)
* Types of analytics (Descriptive, Diagnostic, Predictive, Prescriptive)
* Data-driven decision making and data literacy

---

### 2. Data, Measurement & Reporting

Focuses on how data is organized and used:

* Structured and Unstructured Data
* Data Warehousing and Data Mining
* Dashboards and Data Visualization
* Metrics, KPIs, Benchmarks, and Segmentation

---

### 3. Customer & Sales KPIs

Explores key business performance metrics:

* Conversion Rate
* Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Churn Rate and Retention Rate
* Net Promoter Score (NPS)
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Sales Funnel

This section also includes the **CLV:CAC ratio**, an important indicator of business sustainability.

---

### 4. Marketing & Campaign Analytics

Covers how marketing performance is measured:

* Marketing Campaigns and Post-Campaign Analysis
* Return on Investment (ROI) and Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
* Click-Through Rate (CTR) and Bounce Rate
* A/B Testing
* Lead Generation
* Marketing Attribution

---

## 🛠️ Methodology

Each term in the glossary includes:

* **Definition:** Written in clear, original language (2–3 sentences)
* **Real-World Example:** Showing practical application
* **Source:** Based on credible academic and industry references

The work was researched using reliable sources such as Harvard Business Review, McKinsey & Company, Investopedia, HubSpot, and Google Analytics resources.

---

## 📝 Format Note

This assignment was **completed by hand (pen and paper)** and later **scanned for submission**.
All definitions were written manually to ensure understanding and originality, in line with academic integrity requirements.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Data is most valuable when it is used to support decisions
* KPIs are more important than general metrics because they drive strategy
* Customer-focused metrics (CAC, CLV, churn) are critical for long-term growth
* Marketing success depends on measurement, testing, and attribution
